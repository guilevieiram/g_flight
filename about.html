About this app

Our purpose is to help people keep track of all the good flight deals and plan out their vaccations knowing that they got the best prices available!

Uppon subscription you'll receive e-mails from us every time we find a flight on a good budget leaving from your location. The destinations we cover are the most beautiful and interesting places to visit. Also they are the most visited places on the planet!

The app covers more than 30 locations around the world and updates prices several times a week to ensure you get the most up to date experience.

This is an open source project. So, for all the nerds out there, here is the github link to this project's repo. In there you can find information about all the different functionalities we offer and how everything was built from scratch!

Who did this?

Me! Guile Vieira, a 20 year old software developer, studying at École Polytechnique de Paris

My idea for the app started out with some exercises on requests and API's with python. Until I found the Tequila API (powered by Kiwi) that supplies flight data for a ton of cities in the world! Then, I wanted to make this an actual piece of software, with a good architecture that can actually help people all around the world.

I had to learn almost everything I used for this project: FrontEnd development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript), AWS services (RDS, Lambda), REST API (Flask, Python), hosting (Heroku), designing (Figma), and the list goes on...

O often look at this websites and apps and think to myself: "How is that possible??? How can someone build something that big??". So it was quite exciting to face the challange of making something simmilar (in a much smaller scale, of course). And, in the end, is was very pleasent to see it comming to life, up and running on the cloud!

If you want to know more about the project, make sure to check out my github page /guilevieiram and to reach out to me on social media (links in the end of the page)! Your feedback means a lot to me!
